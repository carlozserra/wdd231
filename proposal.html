Creative Graphic Design - Website Plan

Site Name

Name: Creative Graphic Design

This name was selected to clearly represent the nature of the website, which focuses on offering creative and professional graphic design services. It's direct, professional, and easy to remember.

Site Purpose

This website serves as a personal portfolio and service hub for professional graphic design offerings. It will present examples of logo creation, visual identity development, and advertising material design, while also providing contact information and a form for potential clients to request services.

Scenarios

  • How can I view samples of your logo or branding design work?
  • What is the process and pricing for hiring you for a custom design project?
